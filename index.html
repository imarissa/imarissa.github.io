About Me

I spent the beginning of my adult life persuing a career in pharmacy. I earned my bachelor of science in Physiology and started working as a pharmacy technician. My time behind the counter helped me realize I lacked the necessary passion so I forged a new path. This led me to University of Washington Bothell's graduate studies, where I discovered my love for coding and the many facets of software engineering. Through challenging projects and programs that solidified the concepts and skills needed throughout the software engineering processes, I earned my Graduate Certificate in Software Design and Development.
